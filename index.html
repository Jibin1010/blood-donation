🎯 Our Mission

In critical emergencies, every second counts. Finding a blood donor through scattered social media posts is often inefficient and stressful. Our platform streamlines this process by creating a hyper-local network that connects those in need with those ready to help, instantly.

Who We Serve

👨‍👩‍👧 Patients' Families

Instantly broadcast urgent needs to eligible, nearby donors without relying on viral social media luck.

🩸 Volunteer Donors

Receive targeted, localized alerts when someone nearby urgently needs your specific blood type.

🏥 Local Hospitals

Coordinate emergency supplies efficiently and manage local inventory with real-time donor matching.

Why It Matters

By focusing on real-time proximity and strict blood compatibility, we reduce the time it takes to find a donor from hours to minutes. This ensures that healthcare providers can focus on saving lives rather than searching for resources.

How It Works

1

Request

Patient or hospital submits an emergency blood request detailing the requirement.

2

Match

Our system instantly finds nearby donors with compatible blood types.

3

Alert

Eligible local donors receive immediate push notifications on their devices.

4

Connect

Donor accepts the request and is directed to the medical facility to donate.
